  • Dried thyme - 1 tbsp

  • Ground peppercorn - to taste

Lentil Bean Stew



Method


  1. Mix the green lentils with soaked beans, salt, dried thyme, green chillies, bay leaf and the chunk of onion.
  2. Bring to a boil and simmer for 20-25 minutes. Remove the onion and bay leaf.
  3. Add all other ingredients except spinach and lemon juice.
  4. Simmer till vegetables, beans and lentils are tender (about 20 min).
  5. Now add spinach and lemon juice, simmer till spinach wilts. Serve hot.
